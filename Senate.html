Time in the Senate

Joesph Biden entered the 1972 Delaware Senate race as a major underdog. Biden's campaign had almost no money was though to not have a chance at winning.

Biden's victory was viewed to be largly due to his high energy and ability to connect to the voters. He was the sixth-youngest senator in U.S. history, and was the fourth most senior senator at the time of his resignation
